Tips on Art

Ok! If you've seen my thumbnails, you see that I can draw. I draw through "IbisPaintX" avaliable on mobile/ ipads. If I am correct, in Andriod devices too. It's avaliable on windows. You can also install it on your laptop/ computer with Blustacks. My biggest tip to do art or anything that you want is to practice. In the first few tries it won't be perfect but eventually you'll improve. At first, I suggest you to take a base plat for google. Eventually, you will be able to draw figures by yourself!
